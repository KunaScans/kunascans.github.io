Dr. Arunkumar K

Dr. Arunkumar.K is a highly skilled radiologist with over a decade of experience in diagnostic imaging. He specializes in fetal medicine and cross sectional radiology, having received advanced training from prestigious institutions. As the Radiologist of KUNA SCANS AND DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE, he is dedicated to enhancing diagnostic services in Challakere, ensuring high-quality, accessible healthcare.

With a strong academic foundation, Dr. Arunkumar.K has played a pivotal role in transforming KUNA SCANS AND DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE into a leading center for advanced imaging technologies such as Multislice CT, Digital X-ray and Ultrasound. He remains at the forefront of technological advancements in medical imaging, continuously updating his skills to deliver cutting-edge diagnostic services. His contributions extend beyond clinical practice, as he actively participates in professional development and knowledge-sharing initiatives within the medical community.

Education & Training

  • Completed MBBS from Karnataka Medical College and Research Institute, Hubbali.
  • Earned MD in Radiodiagnosis from JJM Medical College, Davangere.
  • Completed a Fellowship in Cross-sectional Imaging and trained in Fetal Medicine at St. John’s Hospital, Bengaluru.
  • Received Radiology Training from prestigious institutions like NIMHANS, KIDWAI.

Areas of Expertise

  • Fetal Medicine & High-Risk Pregnancy Imaging
  • Pediatric / Musculo-Skeletal Radiology
  • Advanced MRI & CT Imaging
  • USG/CT-Guided Procedures

Key Achievements

  • Played an important role in starting KUNA SCANS AND DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE, bringing cutting-edge imaging services to underserved areas.
  • Introduced high-end ultrasound machine, enabling high-resolution fetal imaging in Challakere.
  • Recognized for advancing 3D/4D techniques and fetal imaging in Tier 2 and Tier 3 towns.
  • Installed the region’s first 32-Slice CT Machine in Challakere, revolutionizing diagnostic capabilities.
