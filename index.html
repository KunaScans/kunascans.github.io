ECG (Electrocardiogram)

A quick and non-invasive test that records the electrical activity of the heart to detect arrhythmias, heart conditions, and overall cardiac health.

Starting from ₹200

Spirometry

A pulmonary function test that measures lung capacity and airflow to help diagnose conditions like asthma, COPD, and other respiratory disorders.

Starting from ₹500

2D Echocardiography (2D Echo)

A non-invasive ultrasound test that provides real-time images of the heart’s structure and function, helping in the diagnosis of heart diseases and monitoring cardiac health.

Starting from ₹1200

Audiometry

A hearing test that evaluates your ability to hear different frequencies and volumes, helping to detect hearing loss or other auditory disorders.

Starting from ₹600

Endoscopy

A diagnostic procedure that uses a flexible tube with a camera to view the inside of your digestive tract, helping detect issues like ulcers, inflammation, or tumors.

Starting from ₹1,500

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by clicking the "Book Appointment" button on our website, which will open your email client with our contact details pre-filled. You can also call us directly or visit our centre.

What should I bring for my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID proof, any previous medical reports or prescriptions, and your doctor's referral if required for specific tests.

How long do test results take?

Most blood tests are available within 24 hours. CT scan reports are typically ready within 2-4 hours. We'll inform you of the exact timeframe when you visit.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major health insurance plans. Please check with our front desk staff for specific coverage details and requirements.

Is your facility accessible for disabled patients?

Yes, our facility is fully accessible with wheelchair ramps, accessible restrooms, and dedicated parking spaces for disabled patients.
